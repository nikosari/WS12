Products to get started with


While playing on a digital client is easy as the client does everything for you, playing with physical cards has some considerations to take into account. On this page I will list and display some supplementary products designed to help you manage and protect your collection and products that are often used while playing.

Different colored and styled card sleeves protect your cards from wear and tear during gameplay, but also allow you to play with double-faced cards. There are many brands to choose from, I personally recommend Dragon Shields, but GameGenic has excellent budget sleeves.
Deckboxes are particularly useful for protecting your deck, organizing decks and making sure they stay intact. There are plenty of designs available and even some custom-made premium ones with extra features.
Dice are used a lot to keep track of different variables within the game, such as counters. Its recommended to have atleast a handful of 20- and 6-sided dice whenever playing.
Playmats protect your cards and dictate the play space of the player. Plus, they are another cool way of expressing yourself!
Tokens are cards that don't start in your deck and instead are generated by other cards. If your deck produces a lot of tokens, its important to have token cards as well so all players are sure of whats actually happening.
